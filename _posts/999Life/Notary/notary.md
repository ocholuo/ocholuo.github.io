## Preperation

在华盛顿州（Washington State）成为一名公证人（Notary Public），按照以下步骤操作：

- ✅ 1. 确认资格
  - 您必须满足以下条件：
  - 年满18岁
  - 能够阅读和书写英文
  - 是华盛顿州居民，或在华盛顿州有工作或执业地点

- 📄 2. 准备所需材料
  - submit application:
    - https://professions.dol.wa.gov/s/saw-help
    - need a Secure Access Washington (SAW) login to access your professional and business licensing information.
    - New professional licenses
      - Apply for a New License

  - 填写完整并经过公证的《宣誓书》（Oath of Office）
    - Oath of Office:
    - Complete the Notary Public Commission Application form, which includes an oath of office, and sign it in front of a notary. The notary will then notarize your signature on the form.

  - 签署的 `$10,000` 保证金（Surety Bond）副本

  - 支付 `$40` 的申请费（如申请**电子公证资格**，需额外支付 `$15`）

  - 您可以通过任何在华盛顿州有执照的保险或担保公司购买保证金。费用因公司而异，通常为一次性费用。您可以咨询您的保险代理人或在线搜索以获取更多信息。

- 📝 3. 提交申请
  - 您可以选择以下方式之一提交申请：
  - 在线申请：
    - 通过 SecureAccess Washington (SAW) 账户提交申请，处理速度更快。
  - 邮寄申请：
    - 将完整的申请材料和费用邮寄至：
    - Notary Public Program
    - Department of Licensing
    - PO Box 3777
  - Seattle, WA 98124-3777
  - 处理时间通常为 30 天。请确保申请材料完整，以避免延误。

- 📬 4. 获取公证人证书
  - 一旦您的申请获批，华盛顿州车辆管理局（DOL）将向您邮寄公证人证书。您可以使用该证书订购公证印章或印模。

- 🖋️ 5. 购买公证印章和日志
  - 根据华盛顿州法律，您需要拥有符合规定的公证印章和日志。以下是一些推荐的产品：
  -
  - Washington Notary Stamp `$25.95`
  - Washington Round Notary Stamp `$24.99`
  - Notary Journal Washington State Official Log Book `$5.99`
  - Official Notary Public Journal Hardbound `$34.50`
  - WA Notary Seal and WA Notary Stamp Kit for Washington Notaries `$59.95`
  - 这些产品均符合华盛顿州的公证要求，您可以根据个人偏好选择合适的印章和日志。


## Note

### $10,000 保证金副本（Surety Bond）

- 签署的 `$10,000` 保证金副本（Surety Bond） 是成为华盛顿州公证人（Notary Public）必须提供的一项财务保障证明，其作用是为了保护公众在您执行公证工作时受到损害时能够获得赔偿。

- Surety Bond（保证金）：
  - 不是您交给政府的钱，而是一种三方协议：
  - **您（公证人）**是被担保人。
  - **Surety 公司（担保公司）**是担保人，它向政府或公众保证您会依法履职。
  - 政府或公众是受益人，如果您渎职或违法，他们可以通过这个保证金获得赔偿（最高可达 `$10,000`）。

- ✅ 为什么需要它？
  - 如果您在执行公证职责时犯错、疏忽或从事不当行为，导致某人蒙受经济损失，这个人可以向担保公司索赔。
  - 担保公司赔付后，有权向您追回这笔钱。

- 🛠️ 怎么获得？
  - 向具有合法资质的保险公司或担保公司购买这份保证金（通常是一次性费用，大概 `$30～$100`，不是每年交的保费）。
  - 获得后，签署该文件并提交一份副本（影印件）随申请一起交给华盛顿州车辆管理局（DOL）。

- 📌 记住：这不是保险（您自己得偿付赔款），而是公众的保障措施。

### notary身份有效期

在WA公证人（Notary Public）身份的有效期是4年。
- 从您申请获批之日起算，有效期为 整整4年。
- 到期前，如果您希望继续担任公证人，必须在期满前90天内提交续期申请。
- 如果过期了，您必须重新申请一次，就像新申请一样，包括重新提交 `$10,000` 的保证金。

### E&O Insurance（差错与疏漏保险）
- 这是可选的保险（Errors & Omissions Insurance）。
- 如果你在履行 Notary 工作时因为疏忽出错（比如身份核查不到位，导致文件被滥用），别人可能会起诉你。
- E&O 保险可以帮你承担潜在的诉讼费用或赔偿金。
- 费用通常不贵，保险期和你的 Notary 任期一致。

## ✅ Notary 公证人常见工作内容

1. 确认身份 & 见证签字
   - 核对签署人的 身份证明文件（驾照、护照等）。
   - 确保签署人是自愿签署、没有受到胁迫。
   - 见证文件的签署过程。

2. 执行不同类型的 Notarial Acts
   - 华盛顿州常见的公证行为包括：
     - Acknowledgments（确认签名属实）
     - Jurats（宣誓/保证签名属实，比如用于宣誓书）
     - Oaths & Affirmations（口头宣誓或保证）
     - Copy Certification（证明副本与原件一致）

3. 贷款/房地产文件签署（Loan Signing Agent）
   - 房屋买卖、再融资、抵押贷款等涉及大量文件。
   - 公证人需要见证买卖双方/借款人的签字。
   - 收入较高，一单可达 `$75–$200`。

4. 遗产、医疗、法律文件
   - 遗嘱（Will）、授权书（Power of Attorney, POA）、信托文件。
   - 医疗授权文件（Healthcare Directives）。
   - 律所、医院、养老院经常需要公证人见证签字。

5. 日常/杂项文件
   - 学校文件（学生交换/留学材料）。
   - 租赁合同、合同签署。
   - 移民文件（部分需要）。

✅ 工作形式
- 兼职 / 自由职业（最常见）：移动公证（Mobile Notary），开车去客户家、咖啡店、医院见证签字。
- 远程公证（RON）：通过视频和电子签名平台完成。
- 办公室/全职工作：银行、律师事务所、保险公司、房地产公司内部都有常驻 Notary。

✅ 所需日常工具
- 公证印章（Notary Seal / Stamp）。
- 公证日志（Notary Journal）。
- 身份核对工具（身份证、护照扫描/核对）。
- 如果做贷款签署，还可能需要 便携式打印机 & 扫描仪。

💰 收费方式
- 普通公证：华盛顿州法律规定每份公证可收取 最高 $10（常见是 $5–$10/份）。
- 移动公证：可以加收 差旅费，比如 $25–$50。
- 贷款签署：通常 $75–$200/单，由 Title 公司或签署平台支付。

📌 总结：
- Notary 的核心就是 见证 + 确认身份 + 确认自愿性，
- 日常涉及法律、金融、房地产、医疗等各种场景。
- 做兼职的话，最赚钱的是 Loan Signing Agent，
- 最轻松的是 远程公证（RON）。

### WAC 308-30-010: 总则 (General Provisions)

```bash
Authority.

This chapter implements the revised uniform law on notarial acts, chapter 42.45 RCW.
[Statutory Authority: RCW 42.45.250. WSR 18-12-028, § 308-30-010, filed 5/29/18, effective 7/1/18. Statutory Authority: RCW 42.44.190. WSR 93-05-009, § 308-30-010, filed 2/5/93, effective 3/8/93. Statutory Authority: 1985 c 156 §§ 5 and 20. WSR 85-24-025 (Order PL 571), § 308-30-010, filed 11/26/85, effective 1/1/86.]
```

这整部法规的目的是规定：

- 公证人怎么申请
- 公证人工作流程是什么
- 公证人能做什么
- 公证人不能做什么
- 怎么盖章
- 电子公证、远程公证怎么办
- 什么情况会被处罚

### WAC 308-30-020: Definitions 术语解释

```bash
Definitions.

Words and terms used in these rules have the same meaning as in the Revised Uniform Law on Notarial Acts, RCW 42.45.010.
"Appear personally" means:
(a) Being in the same physical location as another individual and close enough to see, hear, communicate with, and exchange tangible identification credentials with that individual; or
(b) For remote notarial acts, being in a different physical location from another individual but able to see, hear, and communicate with that individual by means of communication technology.
"Commission" is equivalent to the term "license" as defined in RCW 18.235.010(6).
"Department" means the Washington state department of licensing.
"Director" means the director of the department of licensing or the director's designee.
"Electronic journal" means a chronological record of notarizations maintained by a notary public in an electronic format in compliance with these rules.
"Electronic notarial acts" means notarizations or notarial acts with respect to electronic records.
"Electronic notarial certificate" means the part of, or attachment to, an electronic record that is completed by the notary public, contains the information required under RCW 42.45.130 and the notary's official stamp, bears that notary's electronic signature, and states the facts attested to by the notary in a notarization performed on an electronic record.
"Enroll" and "enrollment" mean a process for registering a notary public with a technology provider to access and use a tamper-evident technology in order to perform electronic notarial acts.
"Principal" means:
(a) An individual whose electronic signature is notarized; or
(b) An individual, other than a witness required for an electronic notarial act, taking an oath or affirmation from the notary public.
"Remote notarial act" means a notarization that is performed using audio-video technology that meets the requirements in WAC 308-30-310 that allows for direct interaction between the notary and the individuals that are remotely located.
"Sole control" means at all times being in the direct physical custody of the notary public or safeguarded by the notary with a password or other secure means of authentication.
```

### WAC 308-30-070：公证印章（Seal/Stamp）的要求


### WAC 308-30-130 — Technology Requirements（技术 & 系统要求）

```bash

(1) For every electronic notarial act and remote notarial act, a notary public shall complete an electronic notarial certificate that complies with the requirements of these rules, RCW 42.45.130 and 42.45.140.
(2) An electronic notarial certificate shall be completed at the time of notarization and in the presence of the principal.
[Statutory Authority: RCW 42.45.250. WSR 21-05-039, § 308-30-150, filed 2/11/21, effective 3/14/21; WSR 18-12-028, § 308-30-150, filed 5/29/18, effective 7/1/18. Statutory Authority: RCW 42.44.190. WSR 93-05-009, § 308-30-150, filed 2/5/93, effective 3/8/93.]
```

每一笔电子或远程公证都必须有正规的电子公证证书
必须在公证时当场填写（远程时也必须同时完成）

### WAC 308-30-170 — Electronic Notarial Signature（电子签名要求）

```bash
Electronic notarial signature.

(1) A notary public shall use a tamper-evident technology that complies with WAC 308-30-130 of these rules to produce the notary's electronic signature in a manner that is capable of independent verification.
(2) A notary public shall take reasonable steps to ensure that no other individual may possess or access a tamper-evident technology used to produce the notary's electronic signature.
(3) A notary public shall keep in the sole control of the notary all or any part of a tamper-evident technology whose exclusive purpose is to perform electronic notarial acts.
(4) For the purposes of this section, "capable of independent verification" means that any interested individual may confirm through the department that a notary public who signed an electronic record in an official capacity had authority at that time to perform electronic notarial acts.
[Statutory Authority: RCW 42.45.250. WSR 18-12-028, § 308-30-170, filed 5/29/18, effective 7/1/18. Statutory Authority: RCW 34.05.410 (1)(a) and 34.05.482 (1)(c). WSR 97-10-052, § 308-30-170, filed 5/1/97, effective 6/1/97.]
```

必须使用可 独立验证（independent verification） 的电子签名
必须保证只有公证员本人能接触其电子签名技术
技术只能用于电子公证，不得他用

### WAC 308-30-180 — Electronic Notarial Stamp（电子印章）

```bash
Electronic notarial stamp.

(1) An electronic stamp may be used to authenticate an electronic notarial act if the electronic notarial certificate conforms to the rules set forth in RCW 42.45.130 and 42.45.140.
(2) An electronic stamp of a notary public used to authenticate an electronic notarial act shall conform to RCW 42.45.150 and WAC 308-30-070.
(3) The electronic stamp of a notary public shall be a digital image that appears in the likeness or representation of a traditional physical notary public official stamp meeting the requirements of RCW 42.45.150 and WAC 308-30-070.
(4) The tamper-evident technology used to create a notary public's electronic stamp shall not be used for any purpose other than performing electronic notarial acts under chapter 42.45 RCW and these rules.
(5) Only the notary public to whom the tamper-evident technology is registered shall generate an official stamp.
[Statutory Authority: RCW 42.45.250. WSR 18-12-028, § 308-30-180, filed 5/29/18, effective 7/1/18. Statutory Authority: RCW 34.05.410 (1)(a) and 34.05.482 (1)(c). WSR 97-10-052, § 308-30-180, filed 5/1/97, effective 6/1/97.]
```

必须是电子图像，外观与传统印章一致
必须用防篡改技术生成
只能由该公证员本人生成和使用，不得共享

### WAC 308-30-190 — Notary Journal（公证日志必需）

```bash
Journal of notarial acts required.

(1) A notary public shall record each notarial act in a journal at the time of notarization in compliance with RCW 42.45.180 and these rules.
(2) If a notary public performs notarial acts involving different statements or documents for the same individual on the same date, the notary public may record a single entry in the journal for all of the statements or documents. The entry shall include the number of statements or documents notarized and shall otherwise conform to RCW 42.45.180 and these rules.
(3) The fact that the notary public's employer or contractor keeps a record of notarial acts shall not relieve the notary of the duties required by these rules.
[Statutory Authority: RCW 42.45.250. WSR 18-12-028, § 308-30-190, filed 5/29/18, effective 7/1/18. Statutory Authority: RCW 34.05.410 (1)(a) and 34.05.482 (1)(c). WSR 97-10-052, § 308-30-190, filed 5/1/97, effective 6/1/97.]
```

每一笔公证都必须记录到公证日志
同一天同一人多份文件可合并记录（需标明数量）
雇主保留记录 ≠ 免除公证员个人的记录义务

### WAC 308-30-200 — Journal Format（日志格式）

```bash
Format of journals of notarial acts.

(1) A tangible notarial journal shall:
(a) Be a permanent, bound book with numbered pages; and
(b) Have the capacity to record for each notarial act:
(i) The information required by RCW 42.45.180(4);
(ii) A description of the notary public's method of identifying the principal; and
(iii) The principal's signature, or the signature of an authorized party in compliance with RCW 42.45.070, or a notation in the notary journal that the notarial act was performed via remote notarization.
(2) If a notary public keeps an electronic journal pursuant to RCW 42.45.180(3), the electronic journal shall:
(a) Be maintained only in addition to the tangible journal;
(b) Have the capacity to record the information required for a tangible notarial journal;
(c) Enable access by a password or other secure means of authentication;
(d) Be tamper-evident;
(e) Create a duplicate record of the journal as a backup; and
(f) Be capable of providing tangible or electronic copies of any entry made in the journal.
(3) A notary public's journal is the exclusive property of the notary public, and shall not be surrendered to an employer upon demand or termination, whether the employer paid for the journal or the notary's bond or application fees.
(4) A notary performing remote notarization must maintain a tangible notary journal as required in RCW 42.45.180 and WAC 308-30-190, this section, and WAC 308-30-210. Notaries performing remote notarization are not required to collect and maintain the signatures of the signers when those notarizations were performed remotely. Notaries must note in their tangible notary log that a notarization was performed remotely.
[Statutory Authority: RCW 42.45.250. WSR 21-05-039, § 308-30-200, filed 2/11/21, effective 3/14/21; WSR 18-12-028, § 308-30-200, filed 5/29/18, effective 7/1/18.]
```

纸本日志：

- 必须是有编号的永久装订本
- 必须包含身份证明方式与`签名`（远程公证可不签名）

电子日志：

- 必须额外保存纸本日志（不是替代）
- 必须防篡改、密码保护、可备份

远程公证：

- 必须记录为“remote notarization”
- 不强制收集当事人的签名

🌼 2. 公证人平常要做什么？
主要是：

1. 确认人的身份
   1. 看身份证、驾照、护照，看这个签字的人是不是真人。
2. 他愿意签字
3. 他精神清醒
4. 看着别人签字: 比如文件要签字，公证人要在旁边看着，确认不是别人代签。
5. 在文件上盖章、写日期: 证明签字是真的。
6. 记录一下事情: 写到自己的小本子，像记账一样：谁来、哪天、签了什么。

```bash
          人来找公证人
                  │
                  ▼
       公证人先检查身份证明
                  │
                  ▼
       确认此人精神正常、愿意签字
                  │
                  ▼
       当着公证人的面签字
                  │
                  ▼
    公证人盖章 + 写进自己的记录本
                  │
                  ▼
         收费（最多10美元）
                  │
                  ▼
             公证完成
```
